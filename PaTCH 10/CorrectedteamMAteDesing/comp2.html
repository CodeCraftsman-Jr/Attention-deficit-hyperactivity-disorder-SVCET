Procedure

Each alphabet will be accompanied by a number. At the end of the task, you will see blank spaces where you need to identify and type the alphabet corresponding to the given number. Example: If the number 8 is shown below a blank line, you should write H in the blank space. You can choose your response only once; corrections are not allowed. You must attempt the blanks sequentially; do not skip any blank lines. A maximum of 50 blank lines will be displayed. The task duration is 1 minute.
