Procedure Overview

Alphabets such as P, B, D, Q, R will appear on the screen, one at a time. Each letter will be displayed for 1 second. Press the 'P' key whenever you see the letter P on the screen. Do not press any other key when a different letter appears. A total of 200 letters (stimuli) will be shown. After every 50 letters, the screen will go blank for 30 seconds. This will repeat for 4 sets. The entire session will take approximately 5 minutes.
